“Boonie Bears” is a Chinese animated series produced by Huaqiang Digital Animation Co., Ltd. The series mainly tells the story of two bears, Boonie and Boonie Bear, and their conflicts with a lumberjack named Guangtou Qiang. Guangtou Qiang is a lumberjack who often clashes with Boonie and Boonie Bear because they disrupt his logging work. The series, with its humorous and funny portrayal of the struggles between Boonie, Boonie Bear, and Guangtou Qiang, also conveys some environmental and family values. Since its premiere in 2012, “Boonie Bears” has quickly won over audiences and become one of the most popular animated series in China.
